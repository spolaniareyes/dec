David Echeverry

Assistant Professor of Finance
Facultad de Ciencias Económicas y Empresariales at Universidad de Navarra
Ph.D. Finance and Real Estate, Haas School of Business, University of California, Berkeley

My research deals primarily with fixed income markets, particularly the information frictions that affect them. My secondary field is Behavioral Economics. Some of my recent research deals with collective action problems in the presence of government interventions, measured using economic games in the field.
